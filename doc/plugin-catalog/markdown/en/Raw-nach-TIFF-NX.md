### Raw to TIFF-NX

A PowerShell script to convert a selected Raw picture to TIFF format. The converted picture is automatically opened in *NX Studio* afterwards.

Uses: [Adobe DNG Converter](app://helpx.adobe.com/camera-raw/using/adobe-dng-converter.html) and [ImageMagick](app://imagemagick.org/script/download.php#windows).
